Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account on QuizMaster, we collect your full name, email address, password (securely hashed), and optional educational qualification. As you use the platform, we automatically track your quiz scores, generated topics, completion times, and computed Experience Points (XP) to maintain leaderboard rankings.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is utilized strictly within the platform to:

  • Authenticate your identity securely.
  • Personalize your AI quiz generation parameters (grade and difficulty).
  • Display your username and XP rank on the global leaderboard.
  • Allow administrators to view aggregated engagement metrics.

3. Data Security

We rely on industry-standard encryption. We do not store plain-text passwords. The application database is strictly managed and shielded against unauthorized API access. By design, QuizMaster is an educational tool and does not gather or solicit deeply personal, financial, or sensitive data outside of standard account administration necessities.

4. Third Parties

QuizMaster does not sell your personal data. We utilize third-party APIs (such as Google Gemini AI) exclusively for generating unlinked educational content prompts based on technical parameters (e.g., "10th grade physics"). No personally identifiable information is packaged natively into those external queries.
