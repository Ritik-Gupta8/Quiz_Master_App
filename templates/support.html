Frequently Asked Questions

How do I attempt a quiz?

Navigate to the Generate AI Quiz tool on your dashboard, select your preferred subject and difficulty, and the AI will build your quiz in roughly 10 seconds. You can then begin the timed practice immediately.

Why am I stuck on an endless parsing loop?

Occasionally, the AI API drops the connection or returns an unprocessable structure. Wait a few minutes and refresh.

How is my XP calculated?

Your XP scales up based on the percentage of correct answers achieved, multiplied directly by the quiz difficulty coefficient (Easy = 1.0x, Medium = 1.2x, High = 1.5x). Only your highest scoring attempt per quiz counts toward your overall rank, preventing grind-spamming.
