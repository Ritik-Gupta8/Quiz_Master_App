About QuizMaster

Welcome to QuizMaster, the ultimate dynamic educational platform designed to empower students and educators alike. Developed by Ritik Gupta, QuizMaster leverages the cutting-edge capabilities of Generative Artificial Intelligence to create customized, on-the-fly quizzes tailored exactly to a student's grade level, subject, and difficulty preference.

Our Mission

We believe that learning should not be a static, one-size-fits-all experience. By dynamically generating questions using AI, we ensure that students never face the exact same test twice, fostering genuine comprehension over rote memorization.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Generation: Instantly create quizzes across mathematics, sciences, history, and more in seconds.
  • Rankings & XP: Track your progress over time with our dynamic Experience Points (XP) system and compete on the global leaderboard.
  • Intelligent Time Tracking: Timed assessments help you build exam-level pacing and confidence.
  • Administrative Oversight: Educators and administrators get full top-down analytical views of student performance.
